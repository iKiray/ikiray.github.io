About

Introduction

I am an aspiring IT professional focused on building strong fundamentals in Linux, scripting, networking, and automation. I am working toward roles in help desk, system administration, and eventually DevOps.

Why IT

I am drawn to IT because I enjoy understanding how systems work and solving real technical problems. Troubleshooting, improving workflows, and helping users are skills I want to develop every day. IT also offers a clear path for continuous learning, which keeps me motivated.

Why Linux

I am learning Linux because it runs most servers, cloud infrastructure, and automation tooling, and it is the foundation for the system administration and DevOps work I want to do. Working with the command line, permissions, file systems, and services has already taught me a lot about how computers really work.

Why Scripting and Automation

Bash, Python, and PowerShell help me automate repetitive tasks, reduce manual errors, and manage systems faster. I am learning scripting so I can work with files, users, logs, and configuration in a repeatable way. Even small scripts save time and reduce mistakes.

Target Role

My short-term goal is to move into IT support, help desk, or service desk. From there I want to keep developing system administration, Linux, networking, and automation skills and grow into a DevOps-focused role over time.
